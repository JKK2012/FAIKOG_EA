Classic Pancake Recipe

This easy pancake recipe makes light and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch. Follow these simple steps to enjoy homemade pancakes in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
